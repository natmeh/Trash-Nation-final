Pollution

Water pollution occurs when harmful substances or chemicals contaminate our beautiful oceans, rivers, lakes, or other bodies of water, making the water quality toxic to humans and even animals. The animals in the water would be harmed and also land animals that might be drinking off of these bodies of water.

The majority of the garbage that enters the ocean every year is plastic because unlike other trash, the single-use grocery bags, water bottles, drinking straws, and yogurt containers.

What is being done?

  • 127 countries has put a ban on one use plastic bags. There are few Organizations that help solve the issue of water pollution. One of these organizations is called The Minnesota Pollution Control Agency. This organization help monitor environmental quality, offers technical and financial assistance, and enforces environmental regulations. This organization helps our environment by finding and cleaning up trash that might affect our health and environment.
  • Another organization that helps improve our health and environment is called The Global Programme of Action for the Protection of the Marine Environment from Land-based Activity. This Organization primarily concentrates on the regulating and reducing wastewater in our environment, litter and nutrient loading.
  • The UN Environment supports nature-based water resources management approaches, including water quality, and leads to the theme for World Water Day 2018 and World Water Management Report 2018, organized by UNESCO WWAP (World Water Assessment Programme).
